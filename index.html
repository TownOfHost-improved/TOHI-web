Roles

TOHO has over 350 unique roles ranging in complication and difficulty.

The Roles page is currently under construction. Check back later for when it's complete!

TOHO has over 300 total roles, with different factions of them. Each Faction has their own type based on how that role operates. More information can be found on the Roles page.

Factions include:

  • Crewmates- known as the "good guys". They can win by eliminating all Evils and staying alive.
  • Impostors- a team that hides among the crewmates killing them and everyone else slowly.
  • Neutrals- each Neutral is on their own solo team. Every role has their own varying win conditons, and abilities.
  • Coven- a team much like the Impostors, however has more mind-bending abilities, and passes around the Necronomicon.
  • Add-Ons- roles added on top of an existing role. A player would get these types of roles as an additional role, something that can help or harm them in the game.

Modded Regions

TOHO mainly uses Modded regions to run its lobbies.

As of TOHO v2.4.0, you will be kicked from a Vanilla region lobby with the mod installed. This is on purpose due to numerous bugs and restrictions caused by the new Innersloth anti-cheat, and will be kept this way until Innersloth relaxes their anti-cheat enough to make TOHO playable again.

You can use Modded Regions to play TOHO. Mobile users and PC users can install modded regions. When using TOHO, the modded regions are already installed for you. Unfortunately, console players cannot install modded regions. Some examples of modded regions include:

  • Modded NA
  • Niko EU
  • Any Impostor region

Features

Varying game modes, anomalies, wills, etc... with more to come

The Features page is currently under construction. Check back later for when it's complete!

TOHO has its own unique Game Modes that has a separate functionality to the base Among Us game. They include Free For All, Cops and Robbers, Ultimate Team, and Trick or Treat.

TOHO also has game modifiers that, when enabled, effect the gameplay drastically. Players can write wills and they will be sent to the meeting chat with the Wills game modifier on. With the Anomalies game modifier on, after each meeting a different effect has a chance to happen.
